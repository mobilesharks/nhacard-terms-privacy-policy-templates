“NHA CARD” Privacy Policy

The following Privacy Policy describes the terms under which the Ministry of Health processes personal data collected during the issue, verification and acceptance of the Cabo Verde Digital Certificate, hereinafter referred to as “NHA CARD”.

Under this policy is guaranteed to safeguard the right to protection of all personal data, under Law nº. 133/V/2001 of January 22, as amended by Law No. 41/VIII/2013 of September 17, and Law nº. 121/IX/2021 of March 17 which stipulates the Legal Framework for Protection of Personal Data, provided voluntarily by the holder, User of the mobile application and whose treatment is explicitly authorized by him.

Personal Data:

Pursuant to applicable law, we consider personal data to be all information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (namely, name, civil identification number, health information, location information, specific elements of the natural person's physical, genetic, economic, cultural or social identity, etc.).

For “NHA CARD” assignment the following data are processed:

a) First and last name;

b) Date of birth;

c) Civil Identification Number;

d) Validity of the civil identification document;

e) COVID-19 test result information;

f) COVID-19 vaccination data; and

g) COVID-19 patient recovery information.

Rigths of the owner:

The owners of personal data, according to this policy, are the natural persons to whom the data concern and who hold or intend to hold the “NHA CARD” certificate.

All users/owners have the right to information, access, update, rectify and/or delete their personal data.

Personal data processor:

The Ministry of Health is the entity responsible for the processing of personal data regarding the "NHA CARD" certificate, setting the data collected, the means of processing and the purposes for which such data will be used.

Being responsible for the process, the Ministry of Health:

• Ensures that data processing is done lawfully, transparently, and with respect for the principle of good faith;

• Ensures that personal data is processed only for the purposes for which it was collected or for compatible purposes;

• Does not perform any transmission of data for commercial or advertising purposes.

The Ministry of Health agrees to adopt the necessary security measures in order to guarantee the protection of the personal data being processed, against possible abusive use, or unauthorized access.

Process Purposes:

The processed data is intended only for the purpose of accessing and verifying the information contained in the owner’s certificate, being stored only in the owner’s device, which has full control of them.

Data Protection Officer:

The Ministry of Health has appointed a Data Protection Officer. The Data Protection Officer of the Ministry of Health is available for any questions or complaints through the following email address: jorge.barreto@ms.gov.cv

Notwithstanding the complaints lodged with the Data Protection Officer, the Data Owner may also lodge complaints with the National Commission for Data Protection (CNPD).

Privacy Policy Changes and Updates

This privacy policy, recommended reading carefully, may be changed or updated at any time.

Changes and updates to this Privacy Policy, as well as its publication, are the exclusive responsibility of the Ministry of Health, and such changes and updates will apply to future activities and data collected.

Copyright and Intellectual Property

The content of the application has copyright and related rights, as well as industrial property rights. As such, this content should not be used outside of the conditions provided for in the legislation in force.

The user may copy, import or use information existing in the application, solely for the purposes of issuing, verifying and accepting “NHA CARD” certificates for personal or public use, provided that such use is not for profit or potentially offensive purposes. However, the user must make express mention of the source of the information.

All attempts to alter or upload information or other actions that may affect the integrity of the "NHA CARD" application system are prohibited.

Jurisdiction

For any dispute arising from the interpretation or application of this privacy policy, the parties elect as competent, with express waiver of any other, the district court of Praia
